Thesis:Development of Pawpaw Strand/Glass Fiber Hybrid Reinforced Epoxy Composites for Structural Application.
The research was carried out to produce composites suitable for electronics and automobliles applications. Chemical treatment was carried out to modify the surface morphology of the fiber. The composites were then produced by incorporating the fibers in the polymer matrix. The mechanical properties, wear and water absorption tests carried out on the samples showed that all the properties were enhanced by the reinforcements. The reinforcement content with optimum value was 6 wt.%.
